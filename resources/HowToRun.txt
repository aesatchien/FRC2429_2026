Quick guide for practice bot and comp bot

Open a new terminal (aka the console, it should be at the bottom of the screen)
Type 'git pull' enter
To Sim: Type 'python -m robotpy sim'
To Deploy: Type 'python -m robotpy deploy'
Open a gui window:
    Find the folder gui, it is FRC2429_202x/gui
    Open a terminal
    Type: 'python main.py'
Open FRC Driver Station
